Culminating Assignment

Wondering what that little triangle in the bottom left of your screen is? Click it!

Happi Jayatunge was a beautiful prince who loved music almost as much as he loved life. He was a multitalented musician, artist, model, online influencer, and super sick toy poodle who touched the hearts of thousands if not millions, he will forever be in our hearts. At the ripe age of 18 he explained his desire for a popup MP3 player on his website from 2008, and nowtwo years after his passing I have made his dream a reality. Happi Player is a simple MP3 player based in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript standing in remembrance of our beloved Happi. It allows for easy customization from adding songs, titles, or album covers.
